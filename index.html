Rohi Rapha Corporation

is a manufacturing company for food supplements established in 2006 to keep up with the demands of the growing market for natural and safe Food Supplements. It is positioned to serve local markets for their manufacturing and development needs in Food Supplements.

A company catering to small and medium enterprises, it is a top choice compliant Private Label Contract Manufacturer that offers manufacturing and product development services and works closely with clients to fulfill their product and service needs. Rohi Rapha Corporation has over 18 years of experience in developing and manufacturing food supplement products in powder, liquid, and capsule form and complying with regulatory requirements. The company has multiple compliance certifications such as FDA LTO and cGMP.

It is the second manufacturing company set up by the partners who run and own, Siegranz Chemworks Corporation which has been in the manufacturing industry of Personal Care and Household Hazardous Products for more than 25 years giving it extensive experience in manufacturing. The Plant Site is located at #15 Sapphire St., DBP Farm Road, Pulang Lupa II, Las Piñas City. Activities in the plant include the Manufacturing facilities, Offices, Materials Receiving Area and Warehouse, Quality Assurance Office and a Quality Control Laboratory.

Our Mission

The company’s mission is to promote locally sourced materials which can be used as ingredients for its products and to offer affordable products that would promote a healthier lifestyle and improve the general quality of life without worrying about adverse effects thus natural.

In line with this mission, the company believes in strong quality assurance and compliance for its product developments and manufacturing lines to ensure safety of consumers.

Our Vision

We at ROHI, are committed to continuously develop our skills and expertise in manufacturing quality products that will meet and surpass the needs and demands of our customers. We shall accomplish this by providing a safe and healthy working environment for our employees and the entire society.

We assure the future of our company by consistently maintaining a well experienced technical group, sustaining our financial stability, ensuring high quality standards and world-class acceptance of our products and services which are always paramount. While recognizing our social responsibility, we hope to become an active part in the growth of the Consumer Products Industry and share a joint effort to provide people a comfortable, convenient and ever increasing affluent life.
